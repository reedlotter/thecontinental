The Continental

The Continental is an independent American news platform built on the belief that the American people deserve unfiltered, honest reporting — free from corporate editorial boards, foreign influence, and political gatekeeping.

Our Mission

We aggregate reporting from independent journalists and outlets who prize facts over narrative. Where opinions appear, they are clearly labeled. We believe an informed citizenry is the first line of defense against tyranny.

Editorial Stance

America First. Pro-sovereignty. Anti-interventionist. Pro-free speech. Skeptical of big government and globalist institutions. Rooted in the Christian principles that formed this nation.

What We Are Not

We are not affiliated with any political party, PAC, or foreign government. We take no corporate advertising that influences editorial decisions. We are reader-supported.
